The Spark: What is S.P.O.S?

S.P.O.S. (Smart Parking Optimized Solution) is a system developed to manage and monitor urban parking smartly and efficiently. It uses real-time sensors to detect illegal parking in restricted areas and helps redirect vehicles to nearby legal parking spaces using a digital interface. Instead of punishing drivers immediately, it informs them, gives them time, and helps them correct their action ‚Äî making it a supportive and smart solution.

The Flow: How it Works

As soon as a vehicle enters a No Parking zone, an IR sensor detects its presence. A buzzer and speaker alert the driver instantly, and a QR code is displayed nearby. When scanned, this QR leads to a purpose-built website that starts a 15-minute countdown, shows a warning message, and helps the driver locate available nearby parking spots with Google Maps links. If the vehicle is not removed in time, the system logs the violation for municipal enforcement.

How S.P.O.S. Works

Real-Time Detection

Infrared (IR) sensors detect any vehicle entering a no-parking zone. The system identifies violations the moment they happen ‚Äî accurately and instantly.

Immediate Alert & Warning

A buzzer sounds and a speaker plays a voice message, informing the driver about the violation and guiding them to scan the QR code.

QR Code Scan & Website Access

A QR code placed near the speaker is scanned by the driver, redirecting them to our website, which displays a live countdown and instructions.

Countdown + Parking Guidance

A 15-minute timer begins. The website shows nearby available parking spaces with direct map links, allowing the driver to relocate quickly.

Violation Logging & Enforcement

If the vehicle is not removed within 15 minutes, the system logs the violation and alerts local authorities. In advanced versions, an automated barrier may activate.
